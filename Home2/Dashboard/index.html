What We Do

The Maya Business Manager is a dedicated distributor of Maya Organics representing a specific state in Nigeria or region in any part of Africa. We continuously formulate products and develop utility contents that serve our customers across the following:

WHO IS A BUSINESS MANAGER?

The Business Manager is the topmost distributor channel serving the lower wholesale chain (product managers) and the final consumers within the Maya distribution network.

WHO IS A PRODUCT MANAGER?

The Maya Product Manager is a dedicated sub-distributor of Maya Organics helping to create access to Maya products through their networks and digital channels. As a product manager within the Maya Distribution Network, you sell directly to the final consumer.
