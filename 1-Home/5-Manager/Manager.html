WHO IS A BUSINESS MANAGER?

The Business Manager is the topmost distributor
channel serving the lower wholesale chain
(product managers) and the final consumers within
the Maya distribution network.

WHO IS A PRODUCT MANAGER?

The Maya Product Manager is a dedicated
sub-distributor of Maya Organics helping to create
access to Maya products through their networks
and digital channels. As a product manager within
the Maya Distribution Network, you sell directly to
the final consumer.
